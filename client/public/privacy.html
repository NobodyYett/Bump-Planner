1. Introduction

Welcome to Bloom ("we," "our," or "us"). Bloom is a pregnancy companion mobile application featuring Ivy, an AI assistant designed to provide educational information, emotional support, and organizational tools throughout your pregnancy journey.

This Privacy Policy explains how Zelkz LLC ("Company") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use the Bloom mobile application (the "App"). By using Bloom, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Bloom, you may provide us with:

  • Account Information: Email address and authentication data when you sign in
  • Pregnancy Information: Due date, baby's name, baby's sex
  • Health & Wellness Logs: Mood, energy level, symptoms, and notes you choose to record
  • Journal Entries: Private written reflections and optional images you upload
  • AI Conversations: Questions you ask Ivy and contextual information you choose to provide
  • Appointments: Healthcare appointments you schedule in the app

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service: Deliver personalized pregnancy features, journaling, tracking, and educational responses
  • Automated Insights: Analyze patterns in your entries to generate summaries, trends, and supportive insights
  • Improve the App: Understand usage patterns to enhance features and reliability
  • Communication: Send important service-related updates

4. Partner Features & Data Access

If you choose to connect a partner account, your partner may receive summarized insights derived from your check-in activity (such as general mood or symptom trends).

Your partner does not have access to your private journal entries, notes, images, or raw check-in data.

5. Third-Party Services

Bloom uses trusted third-party services to operate the App:

  • Supabase: Secure database, authentication, and file storage
  • OpenAI: Powers Ivy's AI-generated responses
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and entitlement management
  • Google / Apple: Sign-in authentication services

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit and at rest, strict access controls, and account-based data isolation to protect your information from unauthorized access.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data through the App
  • Delete your account and associated data at any time
  • Request export of your data where applicable

8. Children's Privacy

Bloom is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13.

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@zelkz.com.
